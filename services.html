What I do

I'm a website and application coder, for both what happens behind the scenes and what you see and experience on the front. Some of my previous work includes events management and booking systems, application forms and data collection web apps, insights and analytics as well as various custom content management systems. I have also supported people in setting up, maintaining and updating their websites.

I can help you if

  • you need someone to help you set up your e-shop, blog or portfolio website.
  • Wordpress, Squarespace, Wix, Shopify or other popular platforms are not enough for what you're looking for and your needs are more complex.
  • you or your team find that you're often doing repetitive, time-consuming data-related tasks.
  • you need any sort of website technical support.
